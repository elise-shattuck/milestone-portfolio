About Me

This is where I will have my first paragraph. I will say some nice things about myself and such. I have led a very interesting life, some might say. (Not me, but some.) My feats of self-branding will make you think "Hey! She's cool!"

Having to brand yourself is the curse of the modern age. I had avoided it until now but no one comes out unscathed. Why can't I just be a person? People are difficult to describe succinctly. I am large, I contain multitudes, etc.

This is my third paragraph. Here I will say more cool things about myself and my work. What do I do with my life? No one knows. I like to paint and crochet and do calligraphy. I am adrift but that's okay because I'm still smart and stuff. People are allowed to take breaks, or so I'm told.
